Biography

Ehsan Haghighat is a postdoctoral associate at the Civil and Environmental Engineering Department of Massachusetts Institute of Technology. His research interests include computational solid- and poro-mechanics and multiphase flow in porous media, stochastic modeling and uncertainty quantification of engineering systems, and the use of artificial intelligence tools, including deep learning, in engineering analysis.
